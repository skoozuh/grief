Help & Support

Got questions or need assistance? Here are some common answers and resources.

  • How do I claim my username? Click the "Join Waitlist" button and follow the instructions to reserve your username.
  • Can I embed my music? Yes! You can embed Spotify and SoundCloud tracks easily.
  • How do badges work? Badges show your profile status and achievements, customizable in your settings.
  • Is there a free plan? Yes, the free plan lets you get started with core features immediately.
  • Who can I contact for support? Use the contact link on your profile or email support@grief.lol.
